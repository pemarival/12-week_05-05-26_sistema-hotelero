# Hotel system — Possible system functionality

## 1. Module: Parameterization

### Functionality: Customer management
Allows registering, consulting, updating, and controlling customer information related to booking, stay, product purchase, or service purchase.

### Functionality: Dynamic price management
Allows configuring variable prices by room type, day type, season, holiday, weekday, or special business condition.

### Functionality: Company registration
Allows managing general hotel or hotel company data, including commercial information, contact data, and billing-related information.

### Functionality: Legal information management
Allows registering legal data related to the hotel operation, commercial documents, internal policies, and conditions applicable to the service.

### Functionality: Employee management
Allows managing employee information for reception, operation, maintenance, inventory, billing, or administration processes.

### Functionality: Day type management
Allows classifying the operating calendar into season, holiday, weekday, weekend, or special day to support price rules.

### Functionality: Payment method management
Allows parameterizing payment methods accepted by the hotel for booking, deposit, partial payment, or final invoice.

## 2. Module: Distribution

### Functionality: Branch management
Allows registering and managing hotel branches, including location, operating capacity, and room relationship.

### Functionality: Room management
Allows managing each room, including identification, branch, type, capacity, status, and operational availability.

### Functionality: Room type management
Allows defining room categories, such as single, double, suite, or other commercial classifications.

### Functionality: Room status management
Allows controlling whether a room is available, booked, occupied, under cleaning, blocked, or under maintenance.

## 3. Module: Service delivery

### Functionality: Room catalog query
Allows displaying rooms available for commercial offering, along with their main features, type, and estimated price.

### Functionality: Room availability query
Allows validating whether a room is available for a specific date range.

### Functionality: Room booking management
Allows creating and managing a booking associated with customer, room, dates, price, and booking status.

### Functionality: Room booking cancellation
Allows canceling a booking according to the conditions defined by the hotel.

### Functionality: Check in
Allows registering customer arrival, activating the stay, and updating room status.

### Functionality: Check out
Allows registering customer departure, closing the stay, settling consumption, and updating room status.

### Functionality: Stay sequence
Allows registering events that happen during the stay, including product consumption, service consumption, and operational updates.

### Functionality: Product sale
Allows registering products consumed by the customer during the stay.

### Functionality: Service sale
Allows registering additional services requested by the customer during the stay.

## 4. Module: Billing

### Functionality: Pre billing
Allows generating a preliminary view of amounts related to booking, stay, product, and service before issuing the final invoice.

### Functionality: Partial payment tracking
Allows controlling deposit, partial payment, pending balance, and payment status related to booking or invoice.

### Functionality: Invoice management
Allows issuing and consulting the final customer invoice.

### Functionality: Purchase detail
Allows relating consumed product and service with the corresponding invoice or pre invoice.

## 5. Module: Inventory

### Functionality: Product management
Allows registering product available for sale or internal hotel consumption.

### Functionality: Service management
Allows registering additional service available for customers.

### Functionality: Supplier management
Allows managing supplier related to product supply or service delivery.

### Functionality: Product tracking
Allows controlling product entries, exits, consumption, and traceability.

### Functionality: Availability query
Allows knowing current product or service availability before sale.

## 6. Module: Notification

### Functionality: Promotion
Allows sending commercial information to customers about offers, discounts, or campaigns.

### Functionality: Alert
Allows sending messages related to booking, payment, check in, check out, availability, or updates.

### Functionality: Term and condition
Allows presenting conditions before purchasing product or service.

### Functionality: Customer loyalty
Allows managing communications aimed at retention, benefits, and repeat purchase.

## 7. Module: Security

### Functionality: Person registration
Allows storing base information for people associated with the system.

### Functionality: User registration
Allows creating access credentials to operate the system.

### Functionality: Role management
Allows defining access profiles according to responsibility.

### Functionality: Permission management
Allows controlling authorized actions within the system.

### Functionality: Module management
Allows organizing system functionality by functional domain.

### Functionality: View management
Allows controlling visual or functional access to specific screens.

## 8. Module: Maintenance

### Functionality: Room maintenance due to use
Allows registering maintenance activities derived from normal room use.

### Functionality: Maintenance due to remodeling
Allows blocking and controlling rooms during remodeling processes.

### Functionality: Operational dashboard
Allows viewing availability, maintenance, and room tracking.
